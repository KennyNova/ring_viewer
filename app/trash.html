How to Enter the Masina Diamonds Jewelry Showroom

Welcome to Masina Diamonds, where luxury meets exceptional service in our exclusive showroom, located in the heart of Americas Mart.

To ensure a seamless experience, please note that we operate by appointment only and require advance registration for all guests. Each visitor must be registered with building security, so kindly inform us of everyone who will be attending your appointment.

  1. Step 1: Schedule an Appointment

    We operate by appointment only to provide you with a personalized experience. Schedule your appointment here to secure your spot.

  2. Step 2: Arrive at Americas Mart, Building 3

    Our showroom is located in Building 3 of Americas Mart. Parking is available directly under the building or at nearby lots for your convenience.

  3. Step 3: Enter the Building

    Once inside, head to the 2nd floor and check in with the front desk staff. Don’t forget to bring your driver’s license or a valid ID to obtain a visitor pass.

  4. Step 4: Take the Elevator to the 6th Floor

    Use the elevators to reach the 6th floor. When you exit, take a left and walk down the hall. You’ll find our showroom at the corner, ready to welcome you.
